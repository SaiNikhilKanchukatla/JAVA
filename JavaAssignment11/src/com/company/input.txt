The Land History of Fiji Island. Mr Ram prasad was multiple time millionaire lived in Votualevu, Nadi Fiji. The real estate agent tricked him by selling the burial ground of US, British and other soldiers from World War 2.

Back in 80’s some people say soldiers still seen at night. The grand daughter got sick at age of 4 and doctors couldn’t figure out her sickness but pastor together with priest saved her life after doctors’ gave up.

Till this day nobody knows the number of bodies were buried under the ground.
